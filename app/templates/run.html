About Monte Carlo Simulation

Monte Carlo simulation runs your financial plan through 500 different economic scenarios to assess the probability of success. Each trial uses randomized but historically-informed values for:

  • Stock market returns
  • Bond market returns
  • Inflation rates
  • Economic conditions

The simulation typically takes a few seconds to complete. Results will show your success rate and detailed projections.
